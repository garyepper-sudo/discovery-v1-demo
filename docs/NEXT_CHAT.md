# Discovery
## Sprint 18 Kickoff

Current Sprint:
Sprint 18

Previous Sprint:
Sprint 17 — Complete

---

# Goal

Shift from Engine Development to Product Refinement.

Sprint 17 successfully completed the explainable reasoning architecture.

Sprint 18 focuses on making Discovery feel calm, premium, and executive-first.

The objective is not adding more reasoning.

The objective is improving how reasoning is revealed.

---

# Sprint 18 Priorities

Priority 1

Simplify the Executive Summary page.

Reduce visible information by roughly 40–50%.

The first screen should answer:

What do we understand?

Why does it matter?

What should leadership do next?

Everything else should remain hidden.

---

Priority 2

Create a single exploration flow.

Instead of multiple buttons:

Trace

Reasoning

Organism

Beliefs

Mechanisms

Everything should begin with:

Explore Understanding →

Inside that experience users can progressively explore:

Evidence

Patterns

Mechanisms

Hypotheses

Beliefs

Contradictions

Organism

Trace

---

Priority 3

Improve the Living Organism.

The organism should become the emotional centerpiece of Discovery.

Future improvements include:

• smoother animation

• richer motion

• visible growth

• particle migration

• clustering

• organism "breathing"

without making it visually distracting.

---

Priority 4

Improve Information Hierarchy.

Every screen should follow:

Executive Answer

↓

Supporting Beliefs

↓

Evidence

↓

Reasoning

↓

Raw Information

Executives should never encounter deep reasoning before understanding the conclusion.

---

Priority 5

Prepare for Beta.

After Sprint 18, Discovery should feel like a cohesive product rather than a collection of engine demonstrations.

Focus areas:

• spacing

• typography

• animations

• consistency

• transitions

• interaction polish

---

# Important UX Canon

Always prefer:

less information

better hierarchy

progressive disclosure

clear decisions

single primary action

The engine should become more sophisticated while the interface becomes simpler.

---

1. Begin each sprint in a fresh chat.

5. Prioritize product quality over adding new engine layers.
