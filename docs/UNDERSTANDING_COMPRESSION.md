# Understanding Compression

## Purpose

Compression is the process by which Discovery reduces many organizational understandings into a smaller number of coherent explanatory structures.

Compression improves organizational cognition without losing organizational knowledge.

---

# Why Compression Matters

Organizations generate enormous amounts of experience.

Without compression, Discovery accumulates thousands of disconnected understandings.

Real organizational understanding requires fewer explanations that account for more experience.

Compression therefore becomes a primary cognitive objective.

# Architectural Principle

Every new cognitive layer should emerge naturally from the maturity of the layer below it.

Mental Models should emerge from stable clusters of organizational understanding.

They should never be manufactured directly.
