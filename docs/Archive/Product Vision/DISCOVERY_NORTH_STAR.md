# Discovery North Star

## Mission

Discovery exists to help organizations become progressively more self-aware.

It continuously transforms everyday organizational experience into enduring organizational understanding.

Our goal is not to summarize documents.

Our goal is not to answer questions.

Our goal is not to automate reports.

Our goal is to build and continuously refine an organization's understanding of itself.

---

# What Discovery Is

Discovery is an Organizational Understanding Platform.

Every meeting, board packet, customer interview, strategy discussion, project update, and decision becomes another experience from which the organization can learn.

Rather than treating every investigation as an isolated event, Discovery continuously integrates new experience into a living organizational understanding that evolves over months and years.

This understanding persists even as employees, executives, and strategies change.

Discovery becomes the organization's long-term cognitive memory.

---

# Core Value Proposition

Organizations generate enormous amounts of experience every day.

Most of that experience disappears.

Discovery ensures that organizational learning compounds rather than resets.

It preserves not only what happened, but what the organization has learned about itself.

# The Product Promise

At any moment Discovery should be able to answer:

• What have we learned about ourselves?

• What has changed?

• What has become more certain?

• What has become less certain?

• Which organizational capabilities are strengthening?

• Which organizational constraints are emerging?

• Which persistent organizational dynamics are shaping our future?

---

# Long-Term Vision

Over time Discovery develops an increasingly accurate understanding of how an organization actually behaves.

Rather than storing isolated documents or isolated insights, Discovery builds an evolving model of organizational understanding that grows more complete with every investigation.

The result is an organization that becomes progressively more self-aware.

---

# Guiding Principles

1. Organizational understanding is more valuable than document summaries.

2. Memory should preserve enduring understanding, not transient information.

3. Every new investigation should improve the organization's understanding of itself.

4. Organizational understanding should evolve continuously rather than restart with every investigation.

5. Every durable memory object must be explainable.

6. Contradictory evidence should refine understanding rather than overwrite history.

7. Discovery exists to augment organizational learning, not replace human judgment.

---

# Product Philosophy

Organizations already possess intelligence.

It exists across thousands of conversations, meetings, decisions, successes, failures, and experiences.

Discovery's purpose is to continuously integrate those experiences into a living body of organizational understanding that survives time, personnel changes, and organizational growth.

Our mission is simple:

Transform organizational experience into enduring organizational understanding.
