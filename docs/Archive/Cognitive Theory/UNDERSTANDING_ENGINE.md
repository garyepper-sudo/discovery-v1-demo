# Discovery Understanding Engine

## Purpose

The Understanding Engine transforms raw information into organizational understanding.

Discovery does not summarize information.

Discovery constructs understanding.

---

## Design Principles

Evidence never becomes understanding directly.

Themes emerge from evidence.

Mechanisms explain why.

Beliefs become stable.

Understanding emerges.

Everything is traceable.

Every belief should explain itself.

Every understanding should be reproducible.

---

## Long-term Vision

The engine becomes Discovery's core intellectual property.

The UI visualizes understanding.

The engine creates understanding.
