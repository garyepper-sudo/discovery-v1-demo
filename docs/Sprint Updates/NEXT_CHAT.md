# Product Identity

Discovery maintains a continuously evolving **Organizational Model** of a business.

The Organizational Model represents Discovery's current understanding of:

- organizational reality,
- strategic objectives,
- organizational conditions,
- primary constraints,
- active initiatives,
- executive decisions,
- organizational dependencies,
- operational risks,
- uncertainty,
- historical outcomes,
- and organizational learning.

Every executive assessment, recommendation, simulation, discovery, initiative review, and answer should be grounded in the Organizational Model.

Discovery exists to help executives:

- understand what is happening,
- determine what deserves attention,
- evaluate strategic decisions,
- execute initiatives successfully,
- and continuously improve future decisions.

Discovery is **not**:

- a dashboard,
- a chatbot,
- a reporting platform,
- a document repository,
- or an autonomous executive.

Discovery is an executive operating environment powered by a continuously evolving Organizational Model.
