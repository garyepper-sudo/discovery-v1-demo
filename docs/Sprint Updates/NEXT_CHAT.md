# Product Identity

Discovery maintains a continuously evolving **Operating Model** of an organization.

The Operating Model represents Discovery's current understanding of:

- organizational reality,
- strategic objectives,
- organizational conditions,
- primary constraints,
- executive decisions,
- Executive Work,
- initiatives,
- organizational dependencies,
- operational risks,
- uncertainty,
- historical outcomes,
- executive learning,
- and Persistent Organizational Memory.

Everything Discovery produces originates from the Operating Model.

Every recommendation, simulation, explanation, initiative review, and executive conversation should strengthen the Operating Model.

Discovery exists to continuously improve executive judgment.

Discovery is **not**:

- a dashboard,
- a chatbot,
- a reporting platform,
- a document repository,
- or an autonomous executive.

Discovery is an Executive Operating System powered by a continuously evolving Operating Model.
