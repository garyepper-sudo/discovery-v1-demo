# Product Identity

Discovery is an **Executive Operating System**.

Its purpose is to continuously improve the quality of executive judgment.

Discovery does not replace executive decision-making.

Discovery improves it through a continuously evolving **Operating Model** that represents how an organization actually works.

Discovery is not:

- a dashboard,
- a chatbot,
- a document repository,
- a reporting platform,
- a business intelligence tool,
- a project management system,
- or a static decision engine.

Discovery is an Executive Operating System powered by a living Operating Model.

# Operating Model

The Operating Model is Discovery's foundation.

It is the continuously evolving cognitive representation of how an organization operates.

The Operating Model represents Discovery's current understanding of:

- organizational state,
- organizational conditions,
- organizational mechanisms,
- strategic objectives,
- executive constraints,
- organizational risks,
- active initiatives,
- executive decisions,
- organizational learning,
- confidence,
- uncertainty,
- historical organizational behavior.

Everything Discovery produces originates from the Operating Model.

Including:

- organizational understanding,
- executive attention,
- recommendations,
- strategic alternatives,
- simulations,
- confidence,
- Executive Work,
- initiative tracking,
- reviews,
- organizational learning.

Every capability within Discovery should consult or strengthen the Operating Model before producing executive reasoning.

The product should continuously reinforce:
