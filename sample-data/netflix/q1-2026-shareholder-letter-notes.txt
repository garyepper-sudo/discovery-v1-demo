Company: Netflix
Source: Public-company demo packet, Q1 2026 shareholder letter notes
Netflix reported Q1 2026 revenue growth of 16 percent year over year and operating income growth of 18 percent. Management maintained its 2026 outlook for revenue of $50.7 billion to $51.7 billion and operating margin of 31.5 percent. The letter describes growth focus areas including improving the core entertainment offering, increasing monetization, and expanding the advertising business.
